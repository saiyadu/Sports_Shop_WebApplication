Our Story

IBRAZ SPORTS started its operation in 2002 to produce quality sporting goods. Within a short period IBRAZ SPORTS developed into a vibrant source and expanded rapidly in different units including Sports Wears, Boxing and Martial Arts Equipment, MMA Equipment, and Fitness Equipment.

At IBRAZ SPORTS, our customer relations department is always seeking towards highest standards of customer satisfaction. Moreover, we are strongly committed to full compliance with global human rights and labor standards in the production of all our finished products.
